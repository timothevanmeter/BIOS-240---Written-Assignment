Guidelines for the written assignment

They are worth 20 points-for a very short paper, so we expect the paper to be concise and well-written. Your writing skill is not a criterion for grading in and of itself-but it is much easier to get your point across if you structure your sentences well.

We expect several, reliable references, cited using in-text citations. The exact number is up to you, but Wikipedia, though not forbidden, is not a stellar reference. At least four references, possibly many more, make for good research. Please do not cite online study forums. Please, if you use any text from another source-cite it.

For each assignment, we expect you to respond to the paper in a particular way. Coherent, interesting, and well thought-out responses that reveal an understanding of the paper receive higher grades. You must make it clear that understand the paper, its methods, and its conclusions.

The paper must be on time and no portion may be identical to something submitted by another student.
